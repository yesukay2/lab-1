One thing I love about Ashesi University is their choice of educational system, thus, the liberal art system. In my case for instance, I am majoring in Computer Science but the educational system used in the school requires me to take some courses that are not directly related to Computer Science, but at the same time I am achieving an in-depth knowledge in Computer Science. This type of educational system always reminds me of my version of a very common phrase jack of many, master of one. But for now, i will just list only the Computer Science related coursework.

  • Structures and Algorithms
  • Database Systems
  • Computer Programming for Computer Science
  • Introduction to Computing & Information Systems
  • Web Technologies
  • Intermediate Computer Programming
  • Discrete Structures and Theory
